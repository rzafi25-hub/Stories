On a chilly Wednesday morning, the gravel lot of Kathy's Grocery and Restaurant - a quaint little country store in Clear Fork on Raleigh County's western end - is lined with several large pickup trucks.

This Wednesday Jan. 16, 2019 photo shows Glenn and Kathy Duncan in their store 'Kathy's Grocery and Restaurant' in Clear Fork, W.Va. The business, previously known as Butch's, has been a staple in the Clear Fork area since the 1950s and has been in its current spot since a fire destroyed its previous location across the street in 1957.
